Server-wide Rules

The following server-wide rules apply to all channels. Failure to abide by these rules will result in an immediate server-wide ban. In addition to these rules, individual channels may have their own set of rules.

  1. You will not hold discussions, make requests, or link to anything that violates local or United States law.
  2. You will immediately cease and not continue to access the site if you are under the age of 18.
  3. You will not make comments that are sexual or suggestively sexual involving minors.
  4. You will not take action to disrupt the communication of other users, including flooding and attempts to acquire unauthorized privileges.
